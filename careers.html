WOMEN VOLUNTEERS FOR PEACE (WOVOP). - CALL FOR VOLUNTEERS.

Women Volunteers for peace (WOVOP) is a young women led Organisation that seeks to support and promote peaceful coexistence and leadership among women and girls through partnerships and volunteerism within the lake region of western Kenya. WOVOP is currently welcoming application for interested volunteers within Kisumu county.

WOVOP is committed to offering equal opportunities and all volunteer recruitment decisions will be based on merit, suitability for the role and experience. All volunteer recruitment decisions will not be influenced by race, color, nationality, religion, sex, marital status, disability or age. We fully endorses a working environment free from discrimination and harassment.

Women Volunteers for peace is committed to team spirit, integrity, partnerships and excellence. Your volunteer role will affect the culture that we live for, as we inspire hope, give direction and motivate change for the transformation of societies. Our aim is to equip young leaders especially women for Societal Transformation so as to have a responsible, dignified and peaceful Society.

Eligibility

  1. Resident of Kisumu county
  2. Articulate in English and Kiswahili
  3. Self driven and passionate
  4. Ready to volunteer
  5. Aged between 15 - 25 Years

N/B: Girls and Young women will be given high priority. To apply and join our team of passionate and self driven young leaders, complete the form below, submit your cover letter and Curriculum Vitae.

Volunteers and Internship Program

Volunteers and internship program gives both local and international volunteers / interns opportunity to volunteer various projects. The program search work placement for volunteers within the organization and our partners in Kenya.

Our volunteer programs are highly regarded for safe projects based on attention to detail, affordability, professional field volunteer support and diverse opportunities for cultural immersion. We are committed to our quality and safe volunteer community programs.

Regularly, we review volunteer projects, host families, field support staff and other logistics to ensure our programs are the best they can be. We recruit volunteers to various projects according to their area interest/skills and taste.

We currently have 170local and 5 international volunteers working with WOVOP in different projects across seven sub-counties in western region of Kenya
