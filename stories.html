Sissy Project

Sissy Project did ensure that 30 vulnerable girls from the rural settlements in Kisumu East and West sub counties aged between 13 to 20 years have access to menstrual health commodities, services and correct information on Sexual reproductive health amid Covid-19 pandemic.

The program was implemented during the second and last quarters of 2020 and supported through donations from individuals and groups
